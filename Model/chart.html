Welcome to Skin Cancer Survival Tool

This tool allows doctors to obtain the survival curve of patients suffering from melanoma cancer. Moreover, patients are classified into
three risk groups: low risk, medium risk and high risk. Higher risk patients are expected to have less survival probability than lower or
medium risk patients.Therefore, this tool allows doctors to asses the risk of their patients, and choose the appropiate treatment.
